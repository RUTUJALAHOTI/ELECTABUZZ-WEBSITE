Power Analysis

To estimate total power generated we embedded Peizoelectric transducer plates within one-kilometer stretch single lane road on an highway and calculated the total power generation within this one-kilometer due to this Peizoelectric transducer.

Factors to be considered while electrcity generation

  • Dimensions of road
  • Load intensity due to
    • Average speed of vehicle
    • Rate of traffic
    • friction of coefficient
  • Time duration (elapsed time of power generation)
